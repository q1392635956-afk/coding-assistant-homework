## An Industry I Care About: AI in Healthcare

Healthcare is one of the most data-rich yet underserved industries when it comes to intelligent automation and decision support. From electronic health records to medical imaging and clinical trials, the volume of information clinicians and administrators must process is immense. AI has the potential to reduce diagnostic errors, streamline administrative workflows, and surface insights that would otherwise remain buried in unstructured data — ultimately improving patient outcomes and reducing costs.

## How Generative AI Can Help

Generative AI brings a new set of capabilities to healthcare that go beyond traditional pattern recognition. Large language models can help clinicians draft clinical notes, summarize patient histories, and answer complex medical queries in plain language. They can assist researchers in synthesizing literature, generating hypotheses, and drafting study protocols. For patients, generative AI-powered tools can provide accessible health education and triage guidance. Used carefully and with appropriate human oversight, generative AI has the potential to make healthcare more efficient, equitable, and personalized.
