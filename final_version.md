# AI in Healthcare: Opportunities and Applications

## About Me

I am Tieyuan Qian, a graduate student at Johns Hopkins University studying Information Systems and AI. I am interested in using AI to solve real problems in healthcare and business. I am especially interested in how AI can support better decision-making and improve work efficiency in practice.

## An Industry I Care About: AI in Healthcare

Healthcare is a field I care about because it directly affects people's lives. I think AI can help doctors and hospitals work more efficiently, especially when they need to manage large amounts of data such as medical images and patient records. If used well, AI can reduce repetitive work and help medical staff focus more on patient care.

## How Generative AI Can Help

Generative AI can support healthcare by drafting clinical notes, summarizing patient information, and explaining medical information in simpler language. It can also help patients better understand their conditions and next steps. In my view, generative AI should be used as a support tool for doctors rather than a replacement for human judgment.
